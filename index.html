Welcome to NCI Golf Club

Posted on 27/11/2014 by NCI Golf

We are delighted to welcome you to our newly revamped golf club and website. Over the past few months members have seen great strides in returning our once award winning club back to the levels expected by members and guests alike.

Our restaurant has been improved with a brand new kitchen and dining area. The new menu incorporates all your old favourites and a few new surprises.

The 19th hole bar has reopened and is an ideal location to hold an award ceremony. Remember or chefs are happy to put together party platters for you and your friends.
